Start my RESP

Start my RESP is a unique iniative by SmartSAVER that aims to simplify the process of connecting families to advice and support for setting up their RESP.

For more information about RESP's or choosing the right one, take a look at our information portal.

Do note, due to this being a development demo, some options (such as additional languages) have been disabled.
